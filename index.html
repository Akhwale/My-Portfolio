Hello,

I am Edwin Akhwale, from the Nairobi, Kenya. I am a holder of a bachelor's degree in Information Science. During my final years of studies, I specialized in Informatics. My love for tech has been significant since my childhood. I like to get crafty and see things working. As a result of the same, I am a huge enthusiast of coding. Through my journey of self discovery I have written codes in more than 10 programming languages. My best programming language is JavaScript. I also have great experience working with other programming languages on both small and huge projects.

My career is drawn to the idea of being able to create something from nothing: from a blank screen to a fully functioning application. This is a challenge that I find incredibly stimulating and rewarding. I have been able to bring new ideas to life and be part of larger teams that work together to create something that can potentially be used by millions of people. I am motivated to continue my learning and improve my skills as a software developer. There is an immense amount of knowledge to be gained, and I look forward to pushing myself to continuously expand my capabilities.

You too can join me in this venture to make the world a better place through coding.

Thank you.
